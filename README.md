# 💰 Profit Leak Detection & Margin Intelligence Dashboard

## 🚨 Problem
A $2.3M revenue portfolio was silently losing profit due to:
- Hidden discounting patterns
- Loss-making customers
- Underperforming product categories
- Geographic margin inconsistencies

---

## 🔍 Key Impact

- Diagnosed **$156K annual profit destruction**
- Identified **3 loss-driving sub-categories + 10 states**
- Exposed **28 repeat loss-making customers**
- Flagged a customer with **−99% margin across 14 orders**
- Reduced reporting time from **3 days → real-time dashboards**

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- Power BI (Dashboarding)
- SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
- DAX (Business logic & KPI modeling)

- Pricing correction for Tables sub-category
- Customer-tier restructuring

- Projected recovery: **$45K annually**
- Shift from reactive reporting → proactive decision system

## 📌 Key Insight

> High revenue ≠ High profit  
> Discounts + poor customer quality silently destroy margins
